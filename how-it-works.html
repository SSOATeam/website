Karang connects providers and beneficiaries of interrelated coastal data and analytics.
KARANG’S DECENTRALIZED NETWORK
Every data provider and beneficiary is a node within Karang’s ever-expanding network. Karang’s digital infrastructure forms the network’s highways that connect these nodes together. Data, analysis, and/or compensation flows along these highways.
KARANG’S SMART CONTRACTS
Data providers and beneficiaries interact with the Karang network via smart contracts, little bits of computer code that automatically execute whenever certain pre-specified conditions are met. Each provider/beneficiary has (a) smart contract(s) that specifies their unique data/analytics resources or requirements. Karang has surveys and offers build assistance that walk through the construction process for these smart contracts.
KARANG’S ALGORITHMS
Our specialized algorithms use the information specified in the smart contracts to detect and connect overlaps within our network. This pools similar data/analysis to satisfy particular beneficiaries, and forms payment pools that finance the generation of this data/analysis by providers. This is revolutionary, because it enables our clients to overcome financial and logistical barriers that would otherwise inhibit their ability to access or produce data/analysis. And because our algorithms are constantly running, these pools are being perpetually formed and re- formed, adapting to keep up with ever-changing supply and demand.
SO, WHAT DOES THIS MEAN FOR ME AS A CLIENT?
- Exchanges are guaranteed to match your unique specifications for every use-case because YOU specify your terms and conditions.
- No exchange of fund, no access to data/analysis will be granted until the terms and conditions you laid out are met.
- Data/analysis ownership remains with the creator/analyst. Only access is granted.
- Transactions are direct between beneficiaries and providers; no middlemen.
- Transactions are secure and confidential, yet traceable.
- Data/analysis discovery or finding buyers for your data/services is no longer a laborious, time-consuming process. It’s completely automated. With Karang, you don’t need to do anything, we handle it all!
