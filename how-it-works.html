Karang facilitates direct, traceable, and secure transactions between buyers and sellers of data and analysis, forming a distributed network of digital connections within coastal communities and the seafood industry. These information buyers and sellers specify their needs or offerings, as well as associated terms and conditions within digital “smart contracts.” Then, Karang's algorithms use these specifications to identify overlapping needs/offerings, executing secure and direct information exchanges whenever there is a match. This matching process effectively pools demand for, and supply of, information to achieve economies of scale from within and beyond individual communities.

KARANG’S DECENTRALIZED NETWORK
Every information seller and buyer is a node within Karang’s ever-expanding network. Karang’s digital infrastructure connects these nodes together, transmitting information and compensation according to terms and conditions specified within smart contracts.

KARANG’S SMART CONTRACTS
Information sellers and buyers interact with the Karang network via “smart contracts” -- bits of computer code that automatically execute whenever certain pre-specified conditions are met. Each buyer/seller has unique smart contracts that specify their information needs or offerings. The Karang team can provide smart contract templates and other smart contract assistance.

KARANG’S ALGORITHMS
Our specialized algorithms use smart contract specifications to match buyers to sellers, pooling relevant information to meet buyers’ requests, and payments for the sellers who generate this information. Karang algorithms perpetually form and reform these pools based on network participants, adapting to ever-changing supply and demand. This revolutionary approach enables clients to bypass financial and logistical barriers to critical information.

SO, WHAT DOES THIS MEAN FOR ME AS A CLIENT?
- Exchanges are guaranteed to match your unique specifications for every use-case because YOU specify your terms and conditions. - No exchange of funds or information access will be granted until the terms and conditions you laid out are met. - Transactions are direct between buyers and sellers; no middlemen. - Transactions are secure and confidential, yet traceable. - Information discovery or finding information buyers is no longer a laborious, time-consuming process. It’s completely automated.
