Potential Use-Cases

Harmful Algal Blooms

Problem: Harmful Algal Blooms (HABs) are estimated to cost over $1 billion in global damages each year. How can Karang help communities and businesses to adapt and mitigate the impact of HABs?

Solution: Accurate HAB predictions can help communities and businesses to plan for these events, and potentially reduce their intensity and severity); however, this requires continuous aquatic monitoring, which is expensive and complicated. Karang can gather the necessary funding, manage the movement of aquatic information, and facilitate HAB predictions at a dramatically reduced price! We do this by identifying every qualifying beneficiary who has pre-specified requirements along with their ability/willingness to pay for HAB predictions. By pooling demand from local water quality monitoring agencies, fishers and aquaculturists, coastal resorts and restaurants, or even recreational beach goers and boaters, we can finance the installation and maintenance of a continuous aquatic monitoring program that streams data into a HAB prediction model for participating beneficiaries!

Coupled Coastal Insurance Schemes

Problem: Coastal weather-related losses totaled over $150 billion globally in 2019, and $102 billion of these losses were not even insured. How can Karang help coastal communities protect their assets?

Solution: Healthy coral ecosystems provide roughly $41 billion in global net economic benefits each year, so keeping reefs intact can yield major dividends. Because a key benefit of helathy reefs is their ability to protect coastalines from storm and flood damage, coastal property owners would directly benefit from coral reef insurance (since funds could be used to help restore reefs when damaged). To get every coastal property owner to chip-in, Karang can help couple coral reef insurance with property insurance. By enabling coastal monitoring systems, we can provide insurers the data they need to design parametric insurance schemes for reefs. Then, coastal property owners who opt to purchase reef insurance through Karang can receive a discount on their property insuance, effectively incentivizing coral reef protection and restoration.

Bomb Fishing Prevention

Problem: Bomb fishing is estimated to destroy over $78 billion each year, worldwide. How can Karang help communities discourage this harmful practice and reduce their losses?

Solution: With real-time acoustic monitoring, authorities can easily apprehend bomb fishers on the water. Unfortunately, real-time detection requires an expensive suite of technologies. However, as previously mentioned, Karang aggregates supply, demand, and funding to implement and maintain solutions for a variety of beneficiaries. With adequate technology, data, and algorithmic support, authorities can receive blast alerts, with a timestamp and aproximate GPS coordinates, so they can immediately pursue the perpetrators.
